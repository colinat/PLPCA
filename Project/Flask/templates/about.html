BUSINESS CASE

We seek to extract sentiments from a list of S&P 500 companies’ transcripts via feature engineering of financial/emotional aspects. Machine learning techniques would be deployed to predict and identify aspects as well as key sentiment scores for each of these aspects for unlabelled transcripts. Our trained model could then be used to parse transcripts users upload for aspect and sentiment mining purposes. These sentiment scores can also be used to predict future price movements.

SCOPE

The aim of our system and model is to provide an application that will parse an earnings text transcript uploaded by investors/analysts/banks to quickly identify key aspects and respective sentiment scores, which in turn will be used for their various business purposes. Examples could include forecasting share prices or assessing for major changes in the credit worthiness of companies.

CHATBOT BRIEF

conversational user interface (UI) that is targeted at specific audience (analysts, investors) will be built. This UI will serve to aid users whom are keen to have a quick interaction with an Artificial Intelligence chatbot to query key elements of the firm’s performance being discussed and emotional aspects of the CEO responding to the questions in the conference call for the appropriate answers. These responses to the queries could potentially help users in the above-mentioned use cases, such as to better gauge/predict the firm’s future financial performance, which in turn could affect the share price correspondingly.
